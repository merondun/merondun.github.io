Bioinformatics.

PUZZLER: GENOME ASSEMBLY.

I’m currently developing Puzzler, a high-throughput, fully containerized genome assembler that integrates HiFi and Hi-C data to generate chromosome-level assemblies. Designed for scalability and pangenome projects, it includes built-in quality assessment (e.g. BUSCO), contaminant filtering, and streamlined post-processing.

MEROTHON: EVERDAY -OMICS.

I maintain merothon, a toolkit of high-utility Python scripts for analyzing omics data. It includes pipelines for PCA and LD visualization from VCFs, scaffold-to-chromosome mapping, genotype plotting, permutation tests, ancestral allele assignment, and summary statistics for VCFs and FASTAs. Designed for scalability and reproducibility, it’s installable via mamba and well-suited for population genomics workflows.
