Integrated Genomics.

COMBINING GPS AND GENOMICS.

By integrating GPS tracking with whole-genome sequencing in cuckoos, we explore how multimodal datasets can reveal the evolutionary impact of climate-driven changes in migration. Across 800,000 years, climatic shifts restructured migratory routes and modulated gene flow, contributing to lineage divergence. This approach highlights how coupling movement ecology with population genomics provides a powerful framework to understand how behavioural and environmental dynamics shape speciation.
