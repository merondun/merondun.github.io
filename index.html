My research combines bioinformatics,
evolutionary genomics, and ecology
to uncover the genetic basis of
adaptation and speciation.

Evolutionary Genomics

What genetic mechanisms drive and maintain adaptation—whether to shifting environments or in the co-evolutionary arms race between hosts and parasites?

Bioinformatics

How can we develop tools, workflows, and documentation to simplify and streamline evolutionary and ecological analysis?

Ecological Genomics

How does movement shape evolution, adaptation, and conservation—and how can we reveal this using integrated multimodal GPS, environmental, and genomic data?

Ideas for collaboration?

I welcome collaborative project ideas, especially from spatial ecologists with tissue samples and ecological data, who are interested in uncovering the genetic basis of their study systems.
