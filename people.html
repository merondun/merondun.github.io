Justin Merondun

I am currently a postdoctoral scientist with the United States Dept. of Agriculture - Agricultural Research Service, developing bioinformatic tools for genome analysis while studying the evolutionary history of tropical crops like breadfruit and guava. I did my PhD at LMU Munich under Jochen B. W. Wolf on cuckoo genomics and crow epigenetics, and my MSc under Aaron B. A. Shafer at Trent University in wolf genetics and lynx epigenetics.
